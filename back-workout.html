Back Workout

  • Lat pull down

    • - 6sets Of Inclining Weight
    - 15-> 10-> 10-> 8-> 10-> 5
    - Rest 60-90sec between sets

  • Seated Cable roll underhand grip

    • - 5sets Of Inclining Weight
    - 12-> 10-> 10-> 5;10-> 5;5,4;4,3;3,2;2,1;1(superset)
    - Rest 60-90sec between sets

  • Bench Dumbbell roll

    • - 5sets Of Inclining Weight
    - 15-> 10-> 10-> 5;10-> 5;10
    - Rest 90-120sec between sets

  • Close grip lat pull down

    • - 4sets Of Inclining Weight
    - 15/15-> 12/12-> 10/10-> 5;10/10
    - Rest 60-90sec between sets

  • Deadlift Progression

    • - 5 partial deadlifts (from knee)
    - 5 hanging deadlifts (from mid shin)
    - 5 deadlifts (from floor)
    4 sets: progress in weight at your own rate.
    Its better to stay light with perfect form.

  • Back extension

    • - 5sets Of 20reps
    - Rest 60-90sec between sets

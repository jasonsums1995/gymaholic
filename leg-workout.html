Leg Workout

  • Squats

    • - 7sets Of Inclining Weight
    - 15-> 10-> 10-> 8-> 4-> 5-> 5-> 15
    - Rest 120-180sec between sets

  • Smith machine lunges

    • - 5sets Of Inclining Weight
    - 10reps each leg
    - Rest 90-120sec between sets

  • Leg press

    • - 5sets Of Inclining Weight
    - 20-> 20-> 15-> 10-> 8
    - Rest 90-120sec between sets

  • Hamstring curl

    • - 4sets Of Inclining Weight
    - 10 Full Reps + 10 Parcial Reps
    - Rest 60-90sec between sets

  • Cable glut kick backs

    • - 4sets Of Inclining Weight
    - 15-> 12-> 10-> 5/5+5/5
    - Rest 90-120sec between sets

  • Leg extensions

    • - 5sets Of Inclining Weight
    - 15-> 10;5/10-> 5;10/5-> 5;10/5-> 10;5/10
    - Rest 60-90sec between sets

  • Calf

    • - 6sets Of Inclining Weight
    - 15Reps per set, with 5reps each posture - Toe pointing direction: /\ | | \/
    - Rest 60-90sec between sets

Shoulder Workout

  • Dumbbell shoulder press

    • - 6sets Of Inclining Weight
    - 15-> 10-> 10-> 8-> 6-> 10each arms
    - Rest 60-90sec between sets

  • Single arm fly

    • - 5sets Of Inclining Weight
    - 15-> 12-> 10-> 8-> 5;5+3;5
    - Rest 60-90sec between sets

  • Barbell front raise

    • - 5sets Of Inclining Weight
    - 15-> 5;10-> 3;10-> 10-> 8
    - Rest 60-90sec between sets

  • Cable row face pull

    • - 4sets Of Inclining Weight
    - 15-> 12-> 10-> 8
    - Rest 60-90sec between sets

  • Front plate raise

    • - 4sets Of 15Reps
    - Rest 60-90sec between sets

  • Db lateral raise

    • - 5sets Of 15Reps
    - Rest 30sec between sets
